      How it works

      Team Pool: 10% of Gross Profit from repair upsells goes into the pool each period. Pot is split by how much $GP you contributed.

      60/40: Seller (who spots & closes) gets 60%; Executor (who does the work) gets 40%. Do it all on the same visit → 100%. Truck share: split 30/30 and 20/20 when two on the job.

      Spotter: If you hand the lead to the office or a Senior and the quote wins, you get 20% of that job’s GP.

      Job GP = (Price to Customer) − (Materials) − ($33 × Man-Hours on site).

      Conditions: GP enters when job is completed, invoiced and paid. Tally closes 11:59 PM last Sunday of the fortnight. Min 50% margin; true upsells only. Estimation: within 15% or 20 min of quote. Callbacks void Seller or Executor share. $10 travel fee per unscheduled parts run. Seller shouts parts from their 60%. Lost shares go to CSG.
